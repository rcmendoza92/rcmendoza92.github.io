    Ruler

    timing chart

    Specific timings may be suggested graphically by means of rulers: simple 1-dimensional charts terminated by vertical pipes (|), with units of passive time indicated by dashes (-) & events indicated by X's or saltires.

    Rulers are placed below captions & above each line of script whose events they plot.

    Comment

    note, aside, admonition, cue, observation, citation, conjecture

    Comments may be included under a caption or in isolation.

    They're indicated by beginning a line with 2 dashes (--) followed by a space or by enclosing an entire line in parentheses, and may be used to write out notes in more verbose/ordinary language; as such, they ignore the formatting used for script data.

    Definition

    dictionary entry

    Defines a term.

    Dictionary entries are written in YAML, a data serialization format where tuples are declared in key-value pairs, and values may be separated by commas.

    Definitions must include at least one term, type & gloss, and optionally may include genre, brief & symbol; only one type is allowed per definition.

    Terms & abbreviations may be ordered according to preference; see Config.
