    Ossuary

    2017

    Shrine for a de­ceased DJ in the form of an elabor­ate sound sculpt­ure.

    A mono­lithic black pre­amp flank­ed by two candle-crowned black studio mon­itors; on top of the pre­amp sits a sub­woofer with a re­cessed under­side; in this cavity is placed a bowl of chick­en wing bones & a con­tact micro­phone which records pres­sure values from the sub­woofer's bass pulses; it then sends those values to a visual­izer for Daft Punk's One More Time.

    The harder the bass hits, the more the bowl of bones rattles; the more the bones rattle, the more an image of the dear­ly de­parted is faded in be­hind the ris­ing & fall­ing bars of the histo­gram.

    Arduino · Processing
